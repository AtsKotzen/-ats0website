A glimpse from past

The Growth

The pup grew up to be a wise, benevolent leader. Intelligent, thoughtful. A king with a crown on his head. The other dogs look up to him to guide them. For his wise counsel. And to save the world. Gained much respect and wealth. Sharing knowledge, wealth and wise counsel to any and everyone.

Trust Shattered

Disbelief that so much hard-earned money of millions could be liquidated, without notice.
